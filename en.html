Ekylibre is a farming ERP distributed under GNU Affero GPL v3 license. It includes following modules:

  • Relationship management
  • Accountancy
  • Sales, purchases, and stocks
  • Production management and traceability
  • User-based access control

It's an web application based on Ruby on Rails.
